the process

After delving into the entire city of Eindhoven, we decided upon focusing on the Strijp-S and Strijp-D areas. We found that there was a disconnect between these two creative playgrounds and proposed a form that would connect the two together with attention to the neighborhood of families. These illustrations that I produced demonstrate our experimental architectural dimensions and the spatial considerations that went into modeling the final building.
